The Grid Escape Room

What is this?

Lolipop, the creators of the Bunyadi naked restaurant, asked us to brand and design the UI for their immersive cocktail experience "The Grid". The project required us to create visual identities for two opposing entities. One for a seemingly harmless corporation that was definitely evil, the other for a bootstrap hacker group.

"This is way too blade runner..."

What we did

We opted to focus design on several key screens within the experience. Providing guidance on how styles would translate across displays in all of the rooms. The consumer facing UI felt more friendly and was earlier on in their journey and while players progressed the interfaces became darker and more cluttered.

I created the experiments in Framer Classic to mix video and sound into clickable prototypes. They were based on sketches and written guides from the games designers. We used these to give the developers a clear understanding of how the interactions would really feel.

The Grid opened in October 2018 at a secret location in Southwark.
